## Conversion Goal

Primary goal: convert high-value HVAC leads in Troy, Montgomery, Birmingham, Atlanta, and Columbus.

Highest-priority lead types:
1. System installs
2. System replacements
3. Commercial HVAC work
4. Emergency jobs with high urgency or business downtime
5. Larger duct work/project-based jobs

Lower priority:
- Small tune-ups far outside the core service area
- Low-ticket diagnostics in distant markets unless schedule allows
- Exact-price shoppers without scope details

## Landing Page Strategy

### Positioning

Pink House Heating & Cooling should be positioned as a fast-response HVAC team serving select Alabama and Georgia markets, with a strong emphasis on replacements, installations, commercial systems, and urgent service needs.

Core message:
"Fast HVAC help for homes and businesses across Alabama and Georgia."

Support message:
"Tell us where the job is, what system you have, and how urgent it is. We will help confirm availability and next steps."

### Funnel

1. Visitor lands from search, ad, referral, or social profile.
2. Hero immediately offers call, text, and request-service form.
3. Page qualifies the right jobs without sounding restrictive.
4. Services section pushes installs/replacements/commercial/emergency.
5. Service-area section explains select travel coverage.
6. Form collects enough detail to prioritize and route the lead.
7. Thank-you state sets expectation: urgent issues should call/text.

### Conversion Priorities

Primary CTA:
- Call Now

Secondary CTA:
- Text Us

Tertiary CTA:
- Request Service

Lead-routing logic:
- Emergency/commercial/replacement/install -> high priority
- Troy/Montgomery local repair -> standard priority
- Birmingham/Atlanta/Columbus small job -> qualify before scheduling
- Safety issue -> human escalation

## Lead-Capture Form Fields

### Required Fields

1. Full name
2. Phone number
3. Service address or nearest city/state
4. Residential or commercial
5. Service needed
6. Urgency
7. Brief description

### Recommended Fields

8. Email
9. Preferred contact method: call/text/email
10. Preferred appointment window
11. Is the system working at all?
12. System type: central AC, heat pump, furnace, rooftop unit, mini split, unsure
13. Approximate system age
14. Photos/video upload
15. Is this a replacement/install request?
16. Business name, if commercial
17. Best callback time
